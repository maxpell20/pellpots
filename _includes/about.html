ABOUT

I’d like to share with you my range of pottery which is all handmade by me in my small studio. I’m told there’s a certain quirkiness about my design style which currently includes pots, plaques, house signs and brooches. The  decoration contains coastal scenes, cottages, classic vehicles etc.

I am unable to undertake commissions at the moment but I can personalise my own designs with numbers, names and dates to commemorate a special event.I hope you enjoy browsing and find something you like, but if not thank you for looking anyway.
