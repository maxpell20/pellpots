ABOUT

I make a range of slab built pots house signs and have lots of ways to personalise some of my own designs by adding birth or wedding details. My pots are slab built.

This means I roll out the clay and shape it to whatever design is desired. I add on the clay design. I then create a three dimension appearance by adding more clay.
